      Request geospatial and mapping support

      Please use this form to request advice, maps, data, satellite images, or anything geospatial related.

      Your request will be sent to the Mapping and Geographic Information Centre (MAGIC), who provide geospatial expertise and support within BAS.

      We aim to be in touch within 2 working days to confirm your request or discuss your requirements.
      If you cannot pick a date above, enter a date in the form YYYY-MM-DD.

      If requesting a map, please provide details regarding map size, extent, level of detail shown, etc.

      If requesting data or advice, please provide as much detail as possible.

      You can use Markdown to format your request.
